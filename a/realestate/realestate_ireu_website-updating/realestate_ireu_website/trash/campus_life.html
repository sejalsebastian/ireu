Campus Life

Overview

Alliance University campus provides an ideal environment for young minds to explore new ideas, encourage creativity and independent thinking. It facilitates faculty to engage in research, staff to cultivate empathy towards students, and everyone to develop the twin qualities of life-long learning for yearning for learning.

Department of Student Support Services

The Department of Student Support Services provides students a platform to develop, nurture and strengthen their talents through numerous opportunities for co-curricular and extra-curricular activities that hone talent. Life at the University promotes the spirit of fun, teamwork, and leadership which is intellectually enriching through a plethora of activities that uphold creativity and innovation.  

An array of events are organized through the year for students to take part and enrich their student life on campus. National days of importance and festivals are celebrated with great fervor. Students participate in several inter-university events enabling laurels to themselves and the University. Social outreach programs through volunteering programs, adoption of villages, plantation drives and awareness campaigns are organized periodically.

Cultural activities and budding artists in various genres are identified, encouraged and provided opportunities to exhibit their skills. The University aims at providing well-rounded growth and holistic development to its student community and the activity zone is buzzing with students and their energies create an ambience of fun, frolic and healthy competition, creating individuals who are well prepared to take on life’s nuances.

The Department of Student Support Services encourages students to be actively engaged in different clubs that focus on functional areas or specializations and extra-curricular activities. These clubs enhance the knowledge of students through various activities and events. Very frequently, the students clubs and organizations invite practitioners from industry to visit and to share their experience with the students, to highlight the expectations of industry and also acts as mentors and serve as jury members for the activities and events organized by these forums. The following is a representative list of the student clubs:

Prometheus

Prometheus as a club makes conscious efforts to keep the student community updated on the contemporary events through brainstorming sessions, intellectual panel-discussions, simulations and quizzes. It has been actively engaging in building a strong academic base for finance major students in enhancing their general awareness and ability to interpret current financial news.

Marketrix (Marketing Club)

The word 'marketing' brings forth a lot of images such as advertisements or marketing strategies. This is what Marketrix brings to Alliance University. It conveys education through fun and develops potential candidates for marketing careers with a focus on knowledge while providing tools and encouragement to become world-class marketing leaders. Learning marketing by practice is achieved by Marketrix through organizing events such as 'Retail Mela,' which pool the marketing talents of young vibrant minds. The club also brings innovation and creativity with advertising and promotion campaigns, and an array of activities and events that allows achievement of its mission through seminars, debates and workshops.

Genesis (Organizational Leadership
and Strategy Club)

Genesis organizes events that develop intellect and promote creativity involving participants from cross-specializations, without forgetting the importance of team spirit and leadership, which are the primary building blocks of an organization. Activities organized by the club encourage students to go beyond traditional 'Fun@Work' models and facilitate an understanding of individualism within teamwork, which is fundamental for developing a spirit of free enterprise.

Praxeis (Operations Management Club)

The Praxeis Club aims to assist students to gain a broader understanding of the opportunities, career paths, trends and challenging issues in business. The club intends to achieve its mission through industry interactions, plant tours, workshops, guest lectures and other activities. It is a student-driven club that provides education and tools required to succeed in an operations management career, the club aspires to pave the way for its members to be innovative and competitive to excel in today's business world.

Student Accommodation & Dining

A hostel provides a feeling of a home-away-from-home to students. With this in mind, the University has halls of residence for students with a secure and a comfortable environment. Each hall of residence has a residential warden and assistants, who are supervised by a chief warden and the hostel administration to promptly address the day to day issues of the hostel residents. The Halls of Residence accommodates students in single, double or triple sharing rooms with round-the-clock internet connectivity. The rooms are available on a ‘first-come-first-served’ basis.

The Food Court built to the highest standards of the hygiene serves healthy, sumptuous multi-cuisine meals to students. With four dining areas and spacious seating, the food court provides students an opportunity to engage over a meal.

Transportation

Bus facility is provided to students and staff members. With a fleet of buses that ply to different parts of the city, safety and convenience of University members is accomplished.

Student Wellness

Health Centre

At Alliance University we strongly believe in healthy living. The Health Centre extends services to students and staff with the facility of a resident doctor and a round-the-clock ambulance service for medical emergencies.

Counselling

Counselling helps to maintain and enhance the psychological, emotional, and relational well-being of our students. With trained counsellors, the counselling cell counsels students to help and motivate them and to attend to their overall developmental needs. Consultations with the counsellor are treated with sensitivity, care, and confidentiality.

Sports & Recreation

A fit mind is an active mind. With its state-of-the-art gymnasium, sports facilities - outdoor and indoor games - and the availability of expert gym instructors and sports coaches, the University encourages students to participate in various sports and recreational activities, apart from their regular academic schedules. Facilities for sports, indoor games and areas for recreation and creative pursuits make Alliance University a great place in shaping strong personalities.
